# Healthcare-dashboard
Healthcare Analytics Dashboard(Excel)

Objective: To analyze healthcare dataset from Kaggle an gain insights by highlighting trends and patterns in patients, medical conditions, cost etc



Tools: Excel(Power query, power pivot)


What I did:

⦁	cleaned and normalized dataset(removed duplicates, standardized formats)and created new calculated column.
⦁	created KPI visuals regarding total patients,most common diagnosis,average stay,trend over the years etc.
⦁	built interactive dashboard with slicers and filters
⦁	analyzed trend over the years



Key insights:

⦁	Most common conditions: Obesity,arthritis,hyper tension,diabetes
⦁	Most females have arthritis, whereas most males have diabetes
⦁	Diabetes is the most expensive
⦁	2020 saw most no.of patients and 2024 least
